# Value Proposition Component

Version: 1.0

---

# Purpose

The Value Proposition Component explains why the offer matters.

Its purpose is to translate the offer into a clear, relevant and believable reason to continue.

A strong value proposition answers:

Why this?

Why now?

Why for me?

Why should I trust it?

---

# Core Philosophy

Value is not what the business provides.

Value is what the customer believes they will gain.

The component should connect the offer to the customer's desired outcome.

---

# Component Objective

This component should clarify:

Who the offer helps.

What problem it solves.

What outcome it creates.

Why the approach is different.

Why the customer should care.

# Value Proposition Formula

We help [specific audience] achieve [desired outcome] through [unique mechanism], without [common frustration].

Example:

We help local service businesses turn website visitors into qualified enquiries through conversion-focused messaging, without relying on generic templates.
