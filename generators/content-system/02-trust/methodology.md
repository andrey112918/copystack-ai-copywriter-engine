# Methodology Component

Version: 1.0

---

# Purpose

The Methodology Component explains the strategic thinking behind the delivery process.

Unlike the Process Section, which explains what happens, the Methodology Component explains why each step exists.

Its purpose is to demonstrate expertise, increase credibility and differentiate the business from competitors.

---

# Core Philosophy

Customers trust experts who can explain why.

A methodology transforms execution into expertise.

The goal is not to impress with complexity.

The goal is to make expertise understandable.

---

# Difference Between Process and Methodology

Process answers:

"What happens?"

Methodology answers:

"Why do we do it this way?"

Both components work together.

---

# Component Objective

The Methodology Component should:

Explain strategic thinking.

Differentiate the business.

Increase authority.

Build confidence.

Support premium positioning.

# Differentiation

The methodology should communicate:

What makes this approach different.

Avoid saying:

"Our process is unique."

Instead explain:

Why your approach produces better results.
