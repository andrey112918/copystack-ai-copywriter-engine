# FAQ Component

Version: 1.0

---

# Purpose

The FAQ Component proactively answers the most common questions, objections and uncertainties a customer may have before taking action.

Its purpose is not to provide documentation.

Its purpose is to remove hesitation.

Every answer should increase confidence.

---

# Core Philosophy

Questions rarely appear randomly.

They appear because uncertainty exists.

Every unanswered question increases friction.

Every answered question increases confidence.

---

# Component Objective

The FAQ Component should:

Reduce objections.

Clarify expectations.

Increase trust.

Simplify decision-making.

Support the final CTA.

---

# Trust Questions

Examples

Who will I be working with?

How experienced are you?

Can I see previous work?

How do I know this will work?

These questions should reduce uncertainty.

---

# Process Questions

Examples

What happens after I contact you?

How long does the process take?

What do I need to provide?

How often do we communicate?

The objective is transparency.

---

# Pricing Questions

Examples

How much does it cost?

Do you offer payment plans?

Are there any hidden fees?

Is a deposit required?

Explain pricing honestly.

Never hide important information.

---

# Results Questions

Examples

When should I expect results?

Do you guarantee results?

How is success measured?

Every answer should remain realistic.

---

# Implementation Questions

Examples

How quickly can we start?

Can you work with my existing systems?

What happens if changes are needed?

How much of my time is required?

The objective is reducing perceived effort.

---

# Final Questions

Examples

How do we get started?

What happens after booking?

What is the next step?

These questions should naturally lead into the CTA.

---

# Writing Principles

Every answer should be:

Clear.

Specific.

Concise.

Helpful.

Honest.

Avoid unnecessary detail.
