# Objection Handling Component

Version: 1.0

---

# Purpose

The Objection Handling Component proactively identifies and addresses the concerns that prevent customers from taking action.

Its objective is not to argue with the customer.

Its objective is to replace uncertainty with confidence.

Every objection removed increases the likelihood of conversion.

---

# Core Philosophy

Customers rarely buy without hesitation.

Hesitation is normal.

Every objection represents missing confidence—not resistance.

The role of this component is to create confidence before objections become reasons to leave.

---

# Component Objective

The Objection Handling Component should:

Identify hidden concerns.

Reduce perceived risk.

Clarify misunderstandings.

Increase trust.

Support the final CTA.
