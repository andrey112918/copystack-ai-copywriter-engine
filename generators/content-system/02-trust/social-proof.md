# Social Proof Component

Version: 1.0

---

# Purpose

The Social Proof Component increases trust by demonstrating that other people or organizations have already achieved positive outcomes with the offer.

Its objective is not simply to show reviews.

Its objective is to reduce uncertainty.

People trust evidence more than promises.

---

# Core Philosophy

Claims create curiosity.

Proof creates confidence.

The stronger the proof,

the less persuasion is required.

Every claim should be supported whenever possible.

---

# Component Objective

The Social Proof Component should:

Increase credibility.

Reduce perceived risk.

Validate claims.

Support positioning.

Strengthen confidence before the CTA.
